4) Google Fonts (external resource)

This Website uses Google Fonts loaded from Google domains (e.g., fonts.googleapis.com / fonts.gstatic.com). When your browser loads these fonts, it makes a request to Google servers. This may transmit technical data such as your IP address and browser information as part of the request.

According to Google, the Google Fonts Web API does not set cookies and is designed to limit the collection, storage, and use of end-user data. For details, see: Google Fonts: Privacy and Data Collection.

If you prefer to avoid any third-party requests when visiting this Website, you can block requests to Google Fonts in your browser or network settings. I may switch to self-hosted fonts in the future to further minimize third-party data sharing.
